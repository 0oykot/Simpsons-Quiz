About *The Simpons*

The Simpsons is an American animated TV show created by Matt Groening.

It first aired in 1989 and is known for being the longest-running animated series in U.S. television history.

The show centers around the Simpson family, consisting of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie, who live in the fictional town of Springfield.

The Simpsons is famous for its satirical humor, pop culture references, and commentary on American society.

It blends absurd situations with relatable family dynamics, making it both a critical and popular success over the decades.
